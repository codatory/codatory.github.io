I sort of already started on this project, before I really fully nailed down the
goals and expectations. But that seems to be the way things go with me these
days, so I'm alright with it 🙂.

This year, in September, I'll turn 33. Before then, I want to visit and hike
every one of Indiana's 32 state parks and reservoirs. I might post about some of
them, I might not. This isn't going to be the authoritative list of where I've
been and where I haven't (that's in [tapforms](https://www.tapforms.com/)). But,
sometimes I want to share about a trip and it seems a little silly to just drop
a couple pictures on Twitter and call it a day. So here we are. Hopefully this
is enjoyable and inspirational.
